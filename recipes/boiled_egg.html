Boied Eggs Recipe

Home

Description

This guide entails how to create my second favourite breakfast items. It is a simple recipe that involves cooking an egg to satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 1 Eggs
  • Pot
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Stove

Note that you can scale up the quantities however you need.

Steps

  1. Fill the pot with water and add salt.
  2. Turn on stove and start warming the pot.
  3. Once there is a rolling boil, toss in your egg(s).
  4. Boil for around 10 minutes.
  5. Take them out when you are ready and enjoy!
