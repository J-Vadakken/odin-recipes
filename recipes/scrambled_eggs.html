Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Home

Description

This guide entails how to create one of my favourite breakfast items. It is a simple recipe that involves cooking an egg to satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Half Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Pan
  • Stove

Note that you can scale up the quantities however you need.

Steps

  1. Cut up the onions.
  2. Turn on stove.
  3. Put pan on stove. Add some oil to it and saute the onions.
  4. Once they're brown remove them
  5. Break your two eggs into a bowl. Add salt and Pepper as required. Add the onions to this mix.
  6. Add some more oil onto the pan and pour this mixture onto it.
  7. Slice up the eggs with your spoon and mix it all around the pan.
  8. Cook until no longer runny, and at your desired dryness.
  9. Once the egg is fully cooked, remove from the pan and enjoy!
